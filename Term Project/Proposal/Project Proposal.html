What is your Website About?

I had an idea in my mind for a while now which was a twitter/Instagram based website dedicated solely for fashion. What it would do is create a constant stream of fashion content in which people can use some form of “Like” button. I also want to be able to make use of hashtags so that people can filter out outfits if they wanted to look for something specific.

What is the Objective of your Website?

Fashion is constantly changing and sometimes there are people who might catch on a bit slow. There are also people who might want to keep up to date or gain inspiration from others. The objective will be to create a community of people who has interest in fashion and allow them to share their own outfits with others.

Who is your Target Audience?

Target audience will mainly be people above 18+ who have even the slightest bit of interest in fashion.

What Content do you Plan to Include in Each Page of your Website

  • Home — Where stream of contents from members of the community will be uploaded
  • Profile — Where a user can view their own profile and manage pictures they have uploaded and who they are following or being followed by.
  • Account — Where user can manage privacy settings and link to other social media pages such as Facebook, twitter,
  • Collection — List of top voted outfits for the season. Can be separated by regions since different weathers.
  • News/Blogs — Conceptual, but if the website were to get big enough perhaps a blog or new about recent or current trends and infographics could be a thing.
  • Styles — Shows all sorts of different clothing style and possibly sponsorship from clothing retailers.
